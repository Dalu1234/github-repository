Main Focus

Minimal World Models from Geometry

Building geometry informed world models that learn structure, connectivity, and dynamics directly from constraints rather than large datasets. The goal is to represent objects, scenes, and agents as continuous latent programs that can be reasoned over, simulated, and composed. This work feeds into educational game engines, spatial intelligence systems, and research submissions.
